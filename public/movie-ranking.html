The Best Movies according to Sam

My top 3 movies of all-time

Dune

It has a complex and engaging story: Dune is based on the novel by Frank Herbert and it weaves together political intrigue, religious themes and exploration of human nature.

Rich World-Building: The Dune universe is meticulosly crafted with its own history, cultures, society and technology.

Influential Sci-Fi: it has had a profound impact on the science fiction genre. It introduced many original conepts, such as spice being a valuable resource, sandworms and the concept of prescience. The movie's ability to visually portray these unique elements can contirbute to its appeal.

Alice in Wonderland

The original "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" novel by Lewis Carroll, published in 1865, is a classic work of children's literature that has captivated readers for generations. Its whimsical and imaginative narrative, filled with curious characters and surreal adventures, continues to resonate with audiences of all ages.

The story transports viewers to a fantastical world where logic and reality are turned upside down. The creative and nonsensical nature of Wonderland appeals to people's sense of wonder and allows for endless possibilities in storytelling and visual design.

Lord of the Rings Trilogy

The movies capture the epic scope of Tolkien's world, bringing to life the vast landscapes, diverse cultures, and rich history of Middle-earth. The sense of scale and grandeur is awe-inspiring.

The trilogy's impact on popular culture is profound. It revived interest in fantasy as a genre, inspired subsequent works, and influenced filmmaking techniques.
